Delete Your Data

TeeSheet TC does not require an account and does not store personal data on our servers. All your data is stored locally on your device. Here's how to remove it.

How to delete your data

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or Applications)
  3. Find and tap TeeSheet TC
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data (or Clear Storage)

Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

What gets deleted

  • Favorite courses
  • Search preferences (default radius, time window, sort order)
  • Theme and display settings
  • Ad credit counter

What is NOT stored on our servers

TeeSheet TC does not store any personal data on our servers. Search queries are processed in real time and are not retained. We have no user accounts, no profiles, and no server-side data to delete.

Cancel your subscription

If you have an active TeeSheet TC Pro subscription, clearing app data does not cancel it. You must cancel separately:

  • Android: Open Google Play Store > tap your profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > TeeSheet TC > Cancel
  • iOS: Open Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > TeeSheet TC > Cancel
